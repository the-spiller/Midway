USS Yorktown (CV-5), the flagship of Rear Admiral Frank Fletcher’s Task Force 17, photographed under attack by planes from the Japanese aircraft carrier Hiryū on the afternoon of 4 June 1942. She is hit on the port side amidships by a Japanese Type 91 aerial torpedo.

Note very heavy anti-aircraft fire.

Yorktown is heeling to port and is seen at a different aspect than in other views taken from USS Pensacola (CA-24), indicating that this is the second of two torpedo hits that she received.

U.S. Navy
