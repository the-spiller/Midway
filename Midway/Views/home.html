Japanese aircraft carrier Akagi photographed leaving Celebes Island on 26 March 1942 for attacks on British bases in Ceylon. Other carriers and battleships of the Imperial Navy’s First Air Fleet Kido Butai follow, from front to back: Soryu, Hiryū, Hiei, Kirishima, Haruna, Kongō, Zuikaku and Shokaku. All but the last two would be en route to Midway some eight weeks later.

We see her island and aft flight deck with parked B5N “Kate” torpedo bombers. Rolled mattresses have been lashed to the island to provide extra protection from enemy attack.

Kure Maritime Museum, Hiroshima
