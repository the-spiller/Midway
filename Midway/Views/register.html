U.S. Navy Torpedo Squadron Six (VT-6) Douglas TBD “Devastator” torpedo bombers are prepared for launching on USS Enterprise (CV-6) at about 0730-0740 hours, 4 June 1942. Eleven of the fourteen TBDs launched from Enterprise are visible. Three more TBDs and ten Grumman F4F “Wildcat” fighters must still be pushed into position before launching will begin.

The TBD in the left front is Number Two (Bureau # 1512), flown by Ensign Severin L. Rombach and Aviation Radioman 2nd Class W.F. Glenn. Along with eight other VT-6 aircraft, this plane and its crew were lost attacking Japanese aircraft carriers somewhat more than two hours later.

The destroyer at left is trailing Enterprise in plane guard position.
